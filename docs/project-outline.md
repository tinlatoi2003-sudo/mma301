# De tai de xuat

## Ten de tai

Ung dung quan ly phong tro cho sinh vien va nguoi di lam.

## Doi tuong su dung

- Nguoi tim phong tro
- Chu tro / quan ly phong

## Nghiep vu chinh

- Dang ky / dang nhap tai khoan
- Xem danh sach phong dang cho thue
- Xem chi tiet phong
- Dat lich xem phong
- Quan ly thong tin phong
- Theo doi thong ke tong quan

## Man hinh de xuat

1. Dang nhap
2. Dang ky
3. Trang chu danh sach phong
4. Chi tiet phong
5. Lich hen cua toi
6. Dashboard thong ke
7. Ho so ca nhan

## Luong nguoi dung

1. Nguoi dung tao tai khoan hoac dang nhap
2. App goi API lay danh sach phong
3. Nguoi dung chon 1 phong de xem chi tiet
4. Nguoi dung bam dat lich xem phong
5. Backend luu lich hen vao MongoDB
6. Nguoi dung vao man hinh Lich hen de theo doi trang thai
